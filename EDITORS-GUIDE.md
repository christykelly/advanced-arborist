# Editor's Guide - Advanced Arborist LLC

Welcome! This guide explains how to update your website without touching any code.

## What is the CMS Admin Panel?

Your website has a built-in content editor at **yoursite.com/admin/**. It's a simple dashboard where you can click on things to edit them — no coding required.

**How to log in:** Once your GitHub account is connected (we'll set this up together), you'll click "Login with GitHub" on the admin page.

## What You Can Edit

### Site Settings
- **Phone number** — The phone number displayed on the site
- **Email** — Your contact email
- **Address** — Your business address
- **Service area** — Describe where you work (e.g., "Serving the Greater Midland area")
- **Social media links** — Facebook and Instagram URLs

### Hours / Availability
- **Availability text** — The text shown about your availability (e.g., "We are always open by appointment!")
- **By Appointment toggle** — Shows/hides the "By Appointment" notice
- **Seasonal notes** — Add notes about seasonal availability (e.g., "Limited availability December-February")

### Services
Your three main service offerings. For each one:
- **Name** — The service name
- **Description** — What you offer
- **Image** — The photo shown with the service

You can also **add new services** or **remove** ones you no longer offer.

### Hero Section
The big banner area at the top of your site:
- **Headline** — The main text (currently "Healthy Trees Start Here!")
- **Subheadline** — The smaller text below it
- **CTA button text** — What the button says (currently "Get Started")

### About / Our Story
- **Tagline** — Your company motto (currently "Trees carry a legacy, lets conserve it!")
- **Body text** — A longer description or story about your business

## Tips

- **Adding a new service**: Go to Services, click "Add," fill in the name, description, and upload a photo. It will automatically appear on your site in a new card.

- **Seasonal updates**: Use the "Seasonal notes" field to let customers know about winter hours or busy seasons without changing your main availability text.

- **Changing your headline**: The Hero Section is the first thing visitors see. Keep it punchy and clear about what you do.

- **Photos**: Service photos look best when they're roughly the same size/shape. Landscape orientation works well for service cards.

## What NOT to Change

- **Don't remove all services** — the page will look empty. If you stop offering something, just remove that one service.
- **The "Get Started" buttons** all send emails to your address listed in Site Settings. If you change your email there, all the buttons update automatically.

## Need Help?

Every change is saved to GitHub and can be undone. If something looks off, just reach out and we'll sort it out.
